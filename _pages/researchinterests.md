## Research Interests

My research interests are in differential equations, dynamical systems, and mathematical biology.
More specifically, I am interested in the following topics:

- Dynamical Systems
- Coupled Oscillatory Dynamics
- Reaction-Diffusion Eqautions
- Mathematical Biology

## Current Research

- Global bifurcation structure of coupled oscillator systems
- Existence of semi-waves for the delayed Fisher-KPP equation
- Synchronization of coupled Stuart-Landau networks
- Savanna dynamics with spatially discrete migration effects
